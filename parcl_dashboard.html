Data Science Methodology

Step-by-step pipeline powering the buyer segmentation engine

01
Data Cleaning
Handled missing attributes, normalized categorical labels, removed duplicates. 2,000 clients x 12 features merged with 10,000 property records.
02
Feature Encoding
LabelEncoder applied to client_type, region, acquisition_purpose, referral_channel, country. 10 features selected for clustering.
03
Feature Scaling
StandardScaler normalizes age, satisfaction, property count, price, and floor area to zero-mean unit-variance distributions.
04
K-Means Clustering
Efficient centroid-based clustering. K=4 selected based on elbow method. 10 initializations with k-means++ seeding for stability.
05
Optimal K Selection
Elbow method showed diminishing returns after K=4. Silhouette score of 0.1596 confirms meaningful cluster separation in high-dimensional space.
06
Cluster Interpretation
Each cluster analyzed by investment purpose, geography, loan behavior, demographics, and spend patterns to assign business labels.
